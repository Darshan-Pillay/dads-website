Privacy Notice

Softfinity Consulting (Pty) Ltd — Last updated June 2026

What we collect

When you submit the contact form on our website we collect your name, email address, and the message you write. If you select a technology area, we collect that too.

Why we collect it

Solely to reply to your enquiry. We do not use it for marketing, profiling, or any other purpose.

Where it goes

Your submission is emailed to the Softfinity team via Resend, a transactional email service. It may also be captured in a private team channel for lead-redundancy purposes. In both cases the data is visible only to Softfinity staff.

How long we keep it

Email threads are retained as long as a business relationship exists or for up to 2 years after last contact, whichever is longer. If your enquiry doesn't lead anywhere, we'll delete it within 6 months.

Copies in the private team channel are retained indefinitely unless you request deletion, at which point we will remove them within 10 business days.

Your rights

You can ask us to correct or delete your data at any time. Email christopher.pillay@softfinity.co.za with the subject "Privacy request" and we'll respond within 10 business days.
