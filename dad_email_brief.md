Website Design Brief – Agnostic IT Consulting Company
1. Company Overview
We are an IT consulting company that provides specialized consultants in:

Artificial Intelligence (AI)

Big Data

Cloud Computing

SAP

Microsoft Technologies

Oracle Solutions

Enterprise Architecture

Software Development

We are agnostic — we do not promote certifications, badges, or vendor affiliations. Our strength lies in delivering unbiased, client-focused solutions.

2. Purpose of the Website
Position the company as a trusted provider of expert consultants.

Highlight our agnostic philosophy: solutions tailored to client needs, not vendor agendas.

Showcase the breadth of expertise across multiple technology domains.

Generate leads through clear calls-to-action and contact pathways.

3. Target Audience
Enterprises undergoing digital transformation.

CIOs, CTOs, and IT leaders seeking unbiased expertise.

Organizations needing consultants who integrate seamlessly with internal teams.

4. Key Features
Homepage: Clear tagline (e.g., “Independent Expertise. Tailored IT Solutions.”).

Services Pages: Dedicated sections for each specialization.

About Us: Emphasize agnostic philosophy and people-first consulting approach.

Consultant Profiles: Highlight human expertise and collaboration.

Case Studies: Demonstrate successful, unbiased solutions.

Contact Page: Simple, direct lead capture.

5. Design Style
Professional, modern, and minimalistic.

Neutral, enterprise-friendly color palette (blues, greys, whites, with subtle accents).

Imagery that emphasizes collaboration, people, and technology — not vendor logos or certifications.

Responsive design across devices.

6. Tone & Messaging
Confident, independent, and client-centric.

Stress the agnostic approach: “We recommend what works best for you, not for vendors.”

Human-centered: “Our consultants work closely with your teams to deliver solutions together.”

Calls-to-action: “Connect with a Specialist Today”, “Unbiased Expertise for Your Enterprise.”

7. Technical Requirements
SEO-friendly structure.

Fast, secure, and scalable.
